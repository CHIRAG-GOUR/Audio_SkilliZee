Why Good Posture Matters

Bad posture can cause: chronic back/neck/shoulder pain, tension headaches, muscle fatigue, poor digestion, reduced lung capacity (shallow breathing), poor balance, higher injury risk, and even lower confidence or mood because you feel "small" or tired.

Good posture prevents pain, reduces stress on your spine and joints, improves breathing and oxygen flow, boosts energy levels, supports better digestion, and actually increases confidence and self-esteem — standing/sitting tall makes you feel and appear more powerful, especially when speaking, working, or presenting.

This tool helps you train yourself to maintain better posture and feel more confident in daily life!
